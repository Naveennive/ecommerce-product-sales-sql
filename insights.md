# 📈 Business Insights

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes sales data from an e-commerce business to identify customer purchasing patterns, product performance, and revenue trends using SQL.

---

## Key Findings

### 🛍️ Product Performance

- Identify the top-selling products.
- Find products with no sales.
- Compare product sales across categories.

---

### 💰 Revenue Analysis

- Calculate total revenue.
- Analyze revenue by product category.
- Measure average order value.

---

### 👥 Customer Analysis

- Identify the highest-spending customers.
- Calculate customer lifetime value.
- Rank customers by total purchases.

---

### 📅 Sales Trends

- Analyze monthly revenue.
- Identify peak sales periods.
- Compare sales performance over time.

---

## Skills Applied

- Database Design
- SQL Joins
- Aggregate Functions
- GROUP BY
- Subqueries
- Common Table Expressions (CTEs)

- Business Analytics
